STEAM is an acronym for Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics. It is an educational approach that integrates artistic and creative thinking into the traditional STEM fields to foster skills like problem-solving and critical thinking. 
Science: The natural and physical sciences.
Technology: The application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes.
Engineering: The design and construction of engines, machines, and structures.
Arts: This includes a wide range of creative fields like visual arts, drama, music, and design.
